All About Insidious

Insidious is a creepy horror movie from 2011 that really gets under your skin.
Its about a family that starts experiencing terrifying supernatural events after their son,
Dalton, falls into a deep coma. They soon realize that their house is haunted and that something sinister is after them.
As they dig deeper, they find out there's a whole other dimension called "The Further" thats involved.
The movie is great at building suspense and has some seriously frightening moments.
