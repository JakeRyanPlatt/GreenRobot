A Review OF Insidious

Sheila Silva August 12, 2024

★★★★★

Insidious is a chilling horror movie that grabs your attention from the start and doesnt let go.
The story revolves around the Lambert family, whose lives are turned upside down when their son, Dalton, falls into a deep, mysterious coma.
As they uncover the sinister forces haunting their home, they dive into the terrifying realm of "The Further," where malevolent spirits are waiting.

Some Insidious Trivia & Fun Facts:

  • #1 - The film's low budget of around $1.5 million led to a very tight shooting schedule.
  • #2 - Director James Wan and writer Leigh Whannell came up with the idea for the movie while discussing their favorite horror films.
  • #3 - Patrick Wilson, who played the lead role, was initially skeptical about the film's supernatural elements but was impressed with the final product.
  • #4 - The concept of "The Further" was inspired by Wan and Whannell's interest in the idea of astral projection and the unknown realms beyond our world.
  • #5 - Despite its success, the film's spooky set design and eerie sound effects were created on a modest budget, showing that effective horror can be made with limited resources.
